My hobbies

  • Volleyball

    Volleyball is not just a sport to me; it's a beloved hobby and a way of life. It's the rhythmic sound of the ball bouncing on the court,
    the thrill of a well-executed spike, and the satisfying high-five after a well-fought match. My love for volleyball goes beyond the game itself; it's a source of joy and a true passion.

  • Painting

    Painting is a way to capture emotions and experiences that are often difficult to put into words. It's a means of storytelling without uttering a single sentence,
    a visual diary of my life's journey. With each piece, I pour a piece of my soul onto the canvas, and the result is a reflection of my perspective, my moods, and my aspirations.
